How It Works

Three ways to move inventory, one platform

Your customers choose how they want to participate — direct purchase, auction bidding, or short-term rental. You control the experience.

01

Direct Resale

The simplest path. Customers list pre-owned items at a set price. Buyers browse, purchase, and check out — just like your primary storefront, but for second life products.

02

Auctions

Let the market set the price. Timed auctions create excitement around rare and high-demand pieces, driving engagement and bringing new buyers to your brand.

03

Rentals

Give customers access without ownership. Short-term rentals lower the barrier to trying your brand and create recurring revenue from a single product.
